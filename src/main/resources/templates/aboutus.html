About Integral University

Inspiring Excellence Since 2004

A Legacy of Innovation & Inclusiveness

Founded in 2004, Integral University is the first enacted Minority University in India, located in the heart of Lucknow. With over 120 academic programs and a lush 120-acre campus, it fosters a culture of diversity, value-based education, and academic excellence.

The university was originally established as the Institute of Integral Technology in 1998, and later elevated to university status by the Government of Uttar Pradesh under Act No. 9 of 2004.
